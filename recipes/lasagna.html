Lasagna Recipe

Description

Classic Italian Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food, layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. This traditional dish combines perfectly seasoned ground beef, tangy tomato sauce, and tender pasta sheets, all baked to golden perfection. It’s a timeless recipe passed down through generations—hearty, flavorful, and perfect for sharing with family and friends. One bite and you'll be transported straight to an Italian kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese (or béchamel sauce for a traditional version)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onion and sauté until translucent.
  4. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  5. Add the ground beef and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  6. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15–20 minutes to develop flavor.
  7. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with a pinch of salt and pepper (optional: add a beaten egg for richness).
  8. Lightly grease a baking dish. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
  9. Add a layer of noodles, followed by ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and meat sauce. Repeat layers, ending with meat sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake an additional 15–20 minutes, or until cheese is golden and bubbly.
  11. Let the lasagna rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing. Garnish with chopped parsley if desired.
  12. Serve warm and enjoy!
