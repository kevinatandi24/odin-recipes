Macaroni Recipe

Description

Creamy Macaroni and Cheese is the ultimate comfort dish—rich, velvety, and loaded with cheesy goodness. Made with tender elbow macaroni coated in a smooth cheese sauce, this timeless recipe is quick to prepare and perfect for weeknight dinners, potlucks, or cozy weekends at home. Whether served as a main or a side, every bite delivers warm, melty satisfaction the whole family will love.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk (whole milk preferred)
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika (optional, for extra flavor)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Bread crumbs (optional, for topping if baking)

Steps

  1. Cook the elbow macaroni according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Whisk in the flour and cook for 1–2 minutes to form a roux (a thick paste), stirring constantly.
  4. Slowly pour in the milk while whisking to avoid lumps. Cook for 3–5 minutes, until the sauce thickens.
  5. Reduce heat to low and stir in the shredded cheddar cheese until melted and smooth.
  6. Season the cheese sauce with salt, pepper, and paprika (if using).
  7. Mix the cooked macaroni into the cheese sauce until evenly coated.
  8. Optional: For a baked version, pour the macaroni and cheese into a greased baking dish, top with Parmesan cheese and bread crumbs, and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 15–20 minutes, or until golden and bubbly.
  9. Serve warm and enjoy!
