Chicken Recipe

Description

Succulent Herb-Roasted Chicken is a timeless classic that's bursting with flavor. Marinated in a blend of fresh herbs, garlic, and lemon, this dish delivers juicy, tender meat with a crispy golden skin. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions, it pairs beautifully with roasted vegetables or creamy mashed potatoes. Simple to prepare yet full of rich, comforting taste—this is a recipe you'll come back to again and again.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 1.5 to 2 kg), cleaned and patted dry
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme leaves
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 lemon, zested and halved
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 onion, quartered (for stuffing)
  • 3–4 sprigs of rosemary and thyme (extra for stuffing or garnish)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. In a small bowl, mix the minced garlic, chopped rosemary, thyme, parsley, lemon zest, olive oil, melted butter, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Rub the herb mixture all over the chicken, including under the skin and inside the cavity for maximum flavor.
  4. Stuff the chicken cavity with the halved lemon, onion quarters, and a few sprigs of rosemary and thyme.
  5. Tie the legs together with kitchen twine and tuck the wings under the body to ensure even cooking.
  6. Place the chicken breast-side up on a rack in a roasting pan.
  7. Roast in the preheated oven for about 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until the juices run clear and the internal temperature reaches 75°C (165°F) in the thickest part.
  8. Let the chicken rest for 10–15 minutes before carving to allow the juices to redistribute.
  9. Serve with your favorite side dishes and enjoy!
